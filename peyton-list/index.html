Peyton List (1998 – Present)

Peyton List, born in Florida in 1998 is an actress and model. She is a fraternal twin, her twin brother’s name is Spencer. She shares the same name with another SAG-AFTRA actress and thus uses the middle initial ‘R’ to distinguish. The other Peyton List (actress, born 1986) is typically referred to as the older one, and hails from Boston, Massachusetts.

Peyton R. List gained early recognition for her role on the Disney Channel’s “Jessie” (2011-2015) and its spin-off “Bunk’d” (2015-2018). She’s expanded into appearances like the “Diary of Wimpy Kid” film series (2011-2012) and the “Cobra Kai” TV show (2019-present). Her versatility showcasing herself as a rising talent in the industry.
